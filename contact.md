## Frequently Asked Questions

### What ages do you work with?

We work with children of all ages, typically from Reception through Year 6 and beyond. Our programs are adapted appropriately for different age groups and developmental stages.

### Do you provide all the equipment?

Yes! We bring everything needed for our sessions, including mats, equipment, and any specialized adaptive aids required.

### How much do your programs cost?

Programs are tailored to each school's specific needs, so costs vary. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your requirements.

### Are your coaches qualified?

Absolutely. All Born2Fly coaches are:
- Gymnastics SA qualified
- Background checked (enhanced clearance)
- First Aid certified
- Trained in adaptive and inclusive coaching techniques

### What areas do you serve?

We provide services throughout KwaZulu-Natal province. Contact us to discuss your location.

### Can you work with children with specific disabilities?

Yes! We have experience working with a wide range of abilities and needs, including physical disabilities, developmental delays, sensory processing differences, and behavioral challenges. We adapt our programs to ensure every child can participate meaningfully.

### How long are the sessions?

Session lengths are flexible based on your needs. Typically:
- 45 minutes for younger children
- 60 minutes for older children
- Extended sessions available for camps and special events

### Can you integrate with our curriculum?

Yes! We work closely with schools to complement and support existing curriculum goals, particularly in physical education, motor skills development, and social-emotional learning.
